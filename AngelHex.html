1. In the celestial realms where angels dwell,
We, guardians unseen, do humbly tell,
Our wings of light, our hearts aflame,
Assigned to safeguard in the Almighty's name.

2. Chosen by grace, to Earth we descend,
To shield humanity, our mission to defend,
With eyes that see beyond mortal sight,
Guiding souls through darkness into light.

3. In every heartbeat, in each whispered prayer,
We stand vigilant, ever-present, aware,
Against adversity and unseen strife,
We offer solace, the essence of life.

4. Oh mortal beings, know this truth,
In the darkest hour, in the trials of youth,
We are here, with love so pure and vast,
From the first breath to the very last.
